Video beyond the box

Mozilla Popcorn

Mozilla Popcorn lets you enhance, remix and share web video.

Popcorn is a free, easy-to-use app that lets you combine video with content from the rest of the web—text, links, maps to pictures and live feeds.

Use Popcorn to create your own newscasts, pop-up videos, multimedia reports, fan videos, guided web tours and more. Remix your favorite videos on YouTube or sounds on SoundCloud, add your own comments and links, or drag and drop in content from all over the web—right in your web browser.

The result is a whole new way of telling stories on the web—videos that are dynamic, full of links, and unique each time you watch them. It's video beyond the box.
